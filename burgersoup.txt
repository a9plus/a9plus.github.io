>> title: Burger Soup

Add @ground beef{} to a #pan{} over medium-high heat and cook for ~{7-10%minutes}, breaking it into crumbles with a #spatula{}, until it is completely browned and reaches 160°F.

Heat your #pot{} of @water{} to a boil.

Add @pasta{} to your pot and follow the instructions on the box. Wide and thin pasta works best.

Add @pasta{} and @ground beef{} to a #bowl{} and let a @bouillon{} cube dissolve.

Spread @shredded cheese{} over the #bowl{}.
